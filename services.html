Services

I provide modern, efficient and scalable digital solutions in web, mobile, AI and intelligent automation tailored to your requirements.

Full-Stack Web Development

I develop modern, secure, and fully responsive websites and web applications using a wide range of technologies including React.js, Node.js, PHP Lumen, MySQL, MongoDB, and the MERN stack. I have hands-on experience building real-world systems such as dashboards, management platforms, admin portals, API-driven applications, and business solutions. From frontend design to backend logic and database architecture, I deliver complete end-to-end solutions tailored to your requirements.

Mobile App Development

Using React Native and Kotlin, I create smooth and feature-rich mobile apps. I’ve built real-world apps including job management systems, taxi booking apps, and task management tools with GPS, authentication and cloud integration.

AI & Machine Learning Solutions

I design and develop end-to-end AI solutions using technologies such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, Keras, and OpenCV. My expertise includes building custom deep learning models, computer vision pipelines, intelligent automation tools, pattern recognition systems, and data-driven predictive analytics. I focus on creating scalable, real-world AI applications that enhance decision-making, automate complex processes, and deliver meaningful insights — supported by my IEEE-published research experience.

AI Voice Automation

I build AI-powered voice agents and communication systems using Twilio, LiveKit, Deepgram and Cartesia APIs. These include automated calling systems, speech recognition, voicebots and real-time communication apps.

UI/UX & Frontend Engineering

I design clean, modern and user-centered interfaces. With React, MUI and design best practices, I create visually appealing layouts, smooth interactions and fully responsive designs.
