AI-Driven Vehicle Valuation & Market Analysis System

A complete deep learning and computer vision solution developed to automate vehicle valuation in Sri Lanka, integrating real-time market data, damage detection, repair cost estimation, and sustainability scoring.

This system automates vehicle evaluation using AI models for image classification, segmentation, and market analytics. It provides real-time price predictions based on Sri Lankan market data, enabling accurate and transparent decision-making for buyers and sellers.

  • Lack of organized vehicle condition datasets in Sri Lanka.
  • Handling image quality variations between sellers.
  • Real-time market price fluctuations.
  • Training segmentation models for diverse damage types.

  • Built custom CNN & segmentation models for accurate damage detection.
  • Integrated OCR for number plate recognition.
  • Processed Sri Lankan market data to create dynamic pricing models.
  • Added sustainability scoring based on fuel type & emission data.

Key Features

  • AI-based damage detection & severity scoring
  • Repair cost estimation using CNN regression
  • Market price prediction using real datasets
  • Number plate recognition & vehicle identification (OCR)
  • Eco-friendly vehicle scoring / sustainability index
  • Augmented reality vehicle overlay (optional)
  • Admin panel for dataset management
